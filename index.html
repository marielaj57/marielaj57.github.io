Stop. Think. Connect

Stop. Think. Connect, led by the Department of Homeland Security, is a national public awareness effort amongst the public and private sectors to help America understand the associated risks of using internet. Its goal is to promote the understanding of cyber threats and highlight how online safety is a shared responsibility.
